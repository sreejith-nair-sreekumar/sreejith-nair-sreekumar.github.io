Research Interests

My research focuses broadly on information theory, machine learning, and mathematical statistics.

More specific interests include:

  • High dimensional and non-parametric estimation

  • Distributed statistical inference

  • Privacy and security

  • Resource allocation in wireless networks

Current Affiliation

I am a postdoctoral research associate at Cornell University, hosted by Prof. Ziv Goldfeld. My research is supported by the NSF Grant of TRIPODS Center for Data Science for Improved Decision Making.
